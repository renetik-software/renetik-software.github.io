    Renetik Music Apps - Privacy Policy

    Renetik Music Apps - Privacy Policy

    Welcome to the Renetik Music Apps ! We value your trust and are committed to protecting your privacy.

    1. Overview

    Renetik Music Apps are developed and maintained by Rene Dohan. This apps are distributed via Google Play and are designed to run entirely on your device without requiring any personal information.

    2. Data Collection

    • No Personal Data Collected
      We do not collect, transmit, or store any personally identifiable information (PII).

    • Local Data Only
      Any project files, settings, or content you create stay on your device.

    3. Data Storage & Deletion

    • On-Device Storage
      All user data is saved locally on your Android device.

    • Easy Removal
      To delete all app data, simply clear the app’s data in your device settings or uninstall the app entirely.

    4. Security & Vulnerability Reporting

    We take security seriously. If you discover a vulnerability or any unintended behavior:

    1. Visit our website at www.renetik.com.
    2. Report the issue via the contact form or email us directly.

    Your responsible disclosure helps us keep Renetik Instruments safe for everyone.

    5. Contact & Support

    For questions, feedback, or support:

    • Email: renetik.instruments@gmail.com
    • Website: https://www.renetik.com

    Thank you for using Renetik Music Apps!
    — Rene Dohan, Bratislava, Slovakia
