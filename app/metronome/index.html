    Metronome

    Practice, perform, and sync with precise tempo control, editable rhythm presets, instruments, and MIDI.

    User Guide

    Free App

    Full App Version

    Precise timing for practice and performance

    Keep tempo visible, editable, and ready for live use. Start and stop from one large control, adjust BPM while playing, tap a new tempo, and shape the click into musical patterns that fit the way you rehearse or perform.

    Large BPM controls

    Set tempo from the main screen, change it during playback, or tap a tempo into the Tap BPM dialog.

    Play, stop, mute

    Use a large central transport, right-side volume control, mute behavior, and audio panic when you need silence immediately.

    Time signatures

    Work in Beats or Bars mode with supported bar lengths of 2, 3, 4, 6, and 12 beats.

    Android notification controls

    Control active playback from the system notification with stop, tempo down, tempo up, app, and exit actions.

    Rhythm presets, instruments, and SoundFonts

    Move beyond a plain beep. Choose built-in patterns, save your own versions, assign drum instruments, import SoundFonts, or expand the sound set through the instrument store.

    Built-in and user presets

    Start with patterns such as Closed Hit-Hat & Maracas, Drum & Hat, wood block, hit-hats, square, and metronome click.

    Save edited rhythms

    Create user presets from your changes, then browse them separately from the built-in library.

    Instrument browser

    Select Studio Drums, search instruments, load imported SoundFont instruments, or open the instrument store.

    Sound pack store

    Browse Standard/Jazz kits, Electronic kits, Power kits, Ethno kits, Machine kits, and FX kits.

    Full sequence editing

    Edit the metronome pattern like a compact rhythm sequencer. Use drum-map or note-name lanes, select notes, reshape timing, and preview the result before saving.

    Drum map and note views

    Switch lane labels between drum names and note names while editing musical positions on the grid.

    Note editing tools

    Copy, remove, move, resize, adjust loudness, and quantize selected notes.

    Quantize control

    Snap edits to 1/32, 1/16, 1/8, 1/4, 1/2, or 1 beat.

    Import and export MIDI

    Exchange rhythm sequences as MIDI so your metronome patterns can move between apps and devices.

    MIDI sync and external control

    Connect the metronome with other music apps and MIDI gear. Send sync out, map incoming MIDI to app actions, learn controls, and test mappings before relying on them live.

    MIDI clock and transport

    Send clock sync and start/stop transport to a selected MIDI output device and channel.

    Metronome note output

    Send metronome notes over MIDI for external sound modules, other apps, or synchronized rigs.

    MIDI control mappings

    Map Audio panic, BPM up/down, preset next/previous, start/stop toggle, and volume.

    Learn and test workflow

    Listen for incoming MIDI events, update a mapping, then test it from the control dialog.
