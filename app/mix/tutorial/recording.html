    How to record in Mix

    Open session selector

    Open "Looper Recorder" preset

    Looper Recorder preset view

    Adjust effects

    Long press record button to open "Recorder" view

    Track 1 selected for recording and select overdub, adjust input level

    Start recording and stop before desired beat

    After sequence recorded it starts looping

    Now record another sequence

    Adjust solo / mute / volume

    Add delay (or any) effect to recorded tracks
